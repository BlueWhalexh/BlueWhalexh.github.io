Social Media

  1. From when did you start using social media?

    I started using social media when I was 12 years old. After I finished my primary school education, my parents rewarded me with my first mobile phone, which allowed me to access social media. At that time, I mainly used WeChat and QQ to chat and share my life with friends and family.

  2. Do you think you spend too much time on social media?

    Yes, I do spend a lot of time on social media. I often chat with friends on WeChat and browse videos on Douyin. The design of these platforms makes it easy to get hooked, and sometimes I even spend an entire day on them, especially during holidays.

  3. What do people do on social media?

    I think most people use social media to share snippets of their lives, such as outings and gatherings. Additionally, people enjoy watching short videos, reading interesting news, or exploring topics they are passionate about, like pets, games, and celebrities. Social media has also become an important channel for acquiring information and learning new things.

  4. Do your friends use social media?

    Absolutely, in recent years, people have become inseparable from social media. At university, some teachers even post assignments and course notifications on social media. My friends and I use WeChat to communicate, and I can say that without social media, life would become very inconvenient.

Sunglasses

  1. Do you often wear sunglasses?

    To be honest, I don’t even own a pair of sunglasses. The only time I wore sunglasses was last month during a trip to Beijing. I borrowed a pair from a friend because I thought wearing sunglasses would make me look cooler in photos.

  2. Do you spend a lot of money on sunglasses?

    No, I haven’t spent any money on sunglasses. Even though I could buy a pair at a reasonable price, I don’t feel the need to because I rarely use them. Also, I find wearing sunglasses a bit uncomfortable.

  3. Do you give sunglasses as a gift?

    Actually, I haven’t. I usually don’t consider sunglasses as a gift because choosing the right pair involves considering comfort and style, and a poorly chosen pair might not satisfy the recipient. I prefer to choose other types of gifts.

  4. Why do you wear sunglasses?

    The only time I wore sunglasses was during my trip to Beijing. The sun was extremely bright at noon, and I encountered a beautiful scenery. Without sunglasses, I could barely keep my eyes open, so I borrowed a pair from my friend.

  5. Have you ever lost your sunglasses?

    No, I haven’t, because the only time I wore sunglasses was when I borrowed my friend’s pair. Knowing that I was borrowing them, I was extra careful to protect them and avoid losing or damaging them.

Cakes

  1. Do you like eating cakes or other sweet food?

    Yes, I love eating sweets, especially ice cream and cream puffs. Every time I eat desserts, I feel very happy. During hot weather, having an ice cream is particularly enjoyable and helps me beat the heat.

  2. Are there any traditional Chinese cakes?

    Yes, in my hometown, there’s a very classic and famous traditional cake called pineapple cake. Almost everyone who visits there buys some to taste. It has a pineapple filling and a great texture, making it very popular among tourists.

  3. Did you like to eat cakes as a child?

    Of course, almost all children love sweets, especially cakes. Although I didn’t get to eat cakes very often when I was young, every time I did, it made me extremely happy. It was a special treat.

  4. Can you make cakes?

    No, I can’t make cakes. In fact, I’m not good at cooking in general. I haven’t specifically learned these skills because I’m not really interested in cooking.

  5. Do you like to have some desserts after meals/for dinner?

    Yes, after finishing my studies for the day, I have dinner. Sometimes, I feel like it’s not enough, so I go to the store to buy some desserts. This makes me feel happy and more satisfied.

Colors

  1. What is your favorite color?

    My favorite color is sky blue. Although I can’t explain exactly why, whenever I see the sky or something in sky blue, I feel very relaxed and peaceful.

  2. Do you usually wear clothes in your favorite color?

    Sometimes, but more often, I prefer wearing solid-colored clothes like white and black because they look simpler. I think this matches my aesthetic better.

  3. Are there any colors that have a special meaning in your country?

    Yes, definitely. Red is a color with special significance in China. For example, the national flag is red. Red symbolizes good luck, joy, and prosperity. It is used extensively in important festivals and occasions for decoration.
